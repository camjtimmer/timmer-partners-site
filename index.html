What We Do

More Than a Sale-Leaseback

Every deal is different. Some owners want a clean exit from their real estate. Others want to stay in the deal and share in the upside. Some just need better debt. Timmer Partners structures each transaction around what the owner actually needs — not what fits a template.

Direct Acquisition

You sell. We buy. Clean and simple — ideal for owners ready to monetize and move on, or investors looking to trade.

Sale-Leaseback

Sell your building, stay as the tenant, and convert your equity into working capital without disrupting your operation.

Partnership Sale-Leaseback

Pull out up to 100% of your building's value while keeping ownership in the deal. We buy the real estate, you retain an equity stake, and we split the upside together.

Debt Solutions

We secure and personally guarantee financing on your behalf, giving you access to better terms than you'd get on your own.
