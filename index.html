What We Do

More Than a Sale-Leaseback

Every deal is different. Some owners want a clean exit from their real estate. Others want to stay in the deal and share in the upside. Some just need better debt. Timmer Partners structures each transaction around what the owner actually needs — not what fits a template.

▶

Direct Acquisition

You sell. We buy. Clean and simple — ideal for owners ready to monetize and move on, or investors looking to trade.

↻

Sale-Leaseback

Sell your building, stay as the tenant, and convert your equity into working capital without disrupting your operation.

⚖

Partnership Sale-Leaseback

Keep ownership in the deal. We buy the real estate, you retain an equity stake, and we split the upside together. Best for owners who believe in the long-term value.

★

Debt Solutions

We secure and personally guarantee financing on your behalf, giving you access to better terms than you'd get on your own.

The Process

How a Sale-Leaseback Works

1

We Evaluate Your Property

You share basic info about your building, lease situation, and goals. We give you a no-obligation valuation.

2

We Structure the Deal

Our team builds a custom offer — whether that's a traditional sale-leaseback, a partnership structure, or a debt solution.

3

You Stay, We Close

You sign a long-term lease, stay in your building, and receive your capital at closing. The whole process typically takes 60–90 days.
